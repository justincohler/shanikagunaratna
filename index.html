Shanika Gunaratna is a multimedia journalist and writer based in Chicago. She has experience producing for both TV and radio on topics ranging from education reform to poverty to foreign affairs.

Currently, she produces stories for Chicago Tonight, the Emmy-winning news show on WTTW, the most watched public television station in the country. Shanika has previously worked at WBEZ Chicago Public Radio on the award-winning foreign affairs show Worldview. She graduated from Northwestern University's Medill School of Journalism with a dual degree in journalism and international studies.
